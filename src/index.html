How to make the interpretation of the cards:

The seeker of answers from tarot was traditionally called the questioner or "querent", and the answer the "quesited".We can view this "questioning" as a spiritual journey, allowing divine guidance to emerge. Depending on which cards appear, and our responses to them, we may or may not like the view and sorty in the cards, but can freely decide which visual impressions and messages to keep with us.
Exact interpretation may be coloured as much by personal experience as current mood.
